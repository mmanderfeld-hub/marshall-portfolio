Removable Arm Assembly for 45 Ton Brake Press

Role: Mechanical Engineering Intern

Company: Wilson Trailer Company

Term: Summer 2025

Design details have been limited to respect company confidentiality.

Overview

Designed and implemented a removable arm system to improve tooling access, maintenance efficiency, and operator safety on a 45-ton brake press.

Technical Contributions

  • Mechanical design and prototyping
  • 3D modeling and fit-up verification
  • Manufacturing support
  • Assembly and testing

Challenges

Balancing structural rigidity with ease of removal while maintaining compatibility with existing equipment.

What I Learned

Gained experience in industrial design constraints, manufacturing workflows, and collaboration with production staff.
