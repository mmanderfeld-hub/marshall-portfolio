3D Printed PLA Boat

Role: Designer and Fabricator

Course: Intro to Mechanical Engineering

Term: Fall 2024

Overview

Designed and manufactured a 3D printed boat using PLA under strict material and dimensional constraints. Performance was evaluated through a buoyancy competition based on load capacity relative to structural mass.

Technical Details

  • 3D CAD modeling
  • PLA material selection
  • FDM printing optimization
  • Buoyancy testing

Design Challenges

Balancing structural strength, watertight integrity, and mass minimization to maximize buoyancy ratio.

Results

Achieved 4th place overall based on buoyancy ratio during class competition testing.

What I Learned

Gained foundational experience in design for manufacturing, rapid prototyping, and experimental evaluation.
